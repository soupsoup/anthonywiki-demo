**Diane Kessler** is Chief Content Officer (CCO) at [[pluralsight|Meridian Skills]] and the direct manager of [[anthony-derosa|Marcus Reyes]]. In that role, she is the executive sponsor of Meridian's newsletter program, podcast, and Meridian Wire initiative.

## Role at Meridian Skills

As CCO, Kessler sits on the executive leadership team alongside the CMO, CPO, CFO, and CRO. She owns Meridian's content strategy and is the primary liaison between the editorial team and the broader leadership group. Her relationship with the CMO is particularly important to the newsletter program's distribution ambitions.

## Leadership style

Kessler's communication preferences are metric-driven and succinct. She expects status updates framed around numbers and outcomes, not process, and gives direct reports significant operational autonomy while staying close to strategic direction.

## Strategic vision

Kessler supports [[anthony-derosa|Marcus Reyes]]'s vision of repositioning Meridian Skills as a media company — a technology training platform that produces editorial content worth reading independently of the product.
