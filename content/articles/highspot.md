**Highspot** is a sales enablement platform that Meridian Skills uses to distribute content to its field sales organization. For the newsletter and podcast program, it serves as a centralized location where sales reps can find and share content with prospects and customers.

## Current use

As of early 2026, a newsletter one-sheet covering all three newsletters (SIGNAL, BREACH, ORBIT) lives in Highspot's enablement section. [[josh-burkhead|Devon Marsh]] was also creating enablement documentation for the podcast to be hosted there. The goal is to make Highspot the single source of truth for field-facing content, alongside other campaign material already hosted there.

The platform has a "Digital Rooms" feature that marketing teams use for one-to-many content distribution, with view tracking. There was an early-2026 discussion with the enablement support team about integrating newsletter archives and video embeds into the platform.

## Limitations noted

Highspot can't automatically pull from the Iterable subscriber list due to compliance constraints around combining lists. It also lacks the attribution tracking that Iterable provides — a gap that matters when trying to connect newsletter engagement to deal activity.
