The **Emmy Award** is a U.S. television industry award. DeRosa joined **[[the-daily-show|The Daily Show]]**'s digital team under Baratunde Thurston, who led the department at the time. During **[[anthony-derosa|Anthony DeRosa]]**'s five years as Digital Production Manager there, the digital department he helped build won an Emmy in 2017 for "Between the Scenes" — the first Emmy of the Trevor Noah era — and received further nominations in 2018 and 2019.
