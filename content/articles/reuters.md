**Reuters** is a global news agency. **[[anthony-derosa|Anthony DeRosa]]** joined Reuters as its Social Media Editor after building a following on his Tumblr blog **[[soupsoup|SoupSoup]]** — one of the earliest examples of a wire service hiring directly out of social media.

## Work at Reuters

DeRosa was among the first journalists at a major wire service to systematically fold social platforms into breaking-news coverage. He co-authored **[[the-verification-handbook|The Verification Handbook]]**, hosted a video series called "Tech Tonic," and wrote as a Reuters columnist — see **[[selected-writing|Selected Writing]]** for his bylines.

## Awards

In 2011, DeRosa was awarded "Best Innovation in Storytelling" by Reuters. In 2013, he shared the "Journalist of the Year" award from *El Mundo* with the Knight Foundation's Jennifer Preston, recognizing pioneering use of social media in journalism.
