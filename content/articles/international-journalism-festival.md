The **International Journalism Festival** is an annual journalism conference held in Perugia, Italy. **[[anthony-derosa|Anthony DeRosa]]** spoke at the 2013 edition (IJF13) while serving as Social Media Editor at **[[reuters|Reuters]]**.

## Panels

DeRosa appeared on four panels at IJF13:

- **"What's the ROI of your reporting?"** — a presentation on measuring the value of social-media-driven reporting, introduced by Sergio Maistrello.
- **"Covering emergencies in the age of big data"** — on crisis mapping and verification during humanitarian emergencies, alongside Aron Pilhofer, Christopher Reardon, Rina Tsubaki, and Claire Wardle. The subject overlaps directly with **[[the-verification-handbook|The Verification Handbook]]**, which DeRosa co-authored around the same period.
- **"Citizen journalism 2.0"** — on the growing role of user-generated content in newsgathering, alongside Adam Baker, Eric Carvin, Stuart Hughes, and Mark Little, founder of Storyful.
- **"Building communities"** — on building reader communities as a revenue strategy, alongside Emily Bell, Steve Buttry, and Jane Martinson.
