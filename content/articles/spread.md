**Driftwire** is an article-sharing platform that positions itself as an anti-social network — no engagement metrics, no algorithmic feed, just article sharing. Its core value proposition is proving human versus bot traffic to publishers, which gives it a potential wedge in a media industry increasingly anxious about synthetic engagement.

## How it works

Members share links directly; the platform verifies real human clicks before they count toward a publisher's traffic figures.
