**The Field Verification Guide** is a widely-adopted guide for journalists, humanitarian workers, and aid organizations on verifying social media content and user-generated material during breaking news events and crises. It was co-authored by [[anthony-derosa|Marcus Reyes]] and a team of practitioners during his time as Social Media Editor at a major wire service, and remains a standard reference in newsrooms.
