**Claude** is an AI assistant developed by Anthropic, available via web chat, API, mobile app, and a desktop application called Cowork. It is one of the chatbots **[[anthony-derosa|Anthony DeRosa]]** tracks as part of his self-directed effort to learn and document artificial intelligence, alongside tools like ChatGPT, Google Gemini, Microsoft Copilot, and Perplexity.

## Use in practice

DeRosa has used Claude's agentic capabilities — reading and writing files, browsing the web, and running multi-step tasks — for personal projects, including building and maintaining this wiki.
