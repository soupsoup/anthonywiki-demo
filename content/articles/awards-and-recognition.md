Beyond the **[[emmy-award|Emmy Award]]** won during his time at **[[the-daily-show|The Daily Show]]**, **[[anthony-derosa|Anthony DeRosa]]** received a number of awards and press mentions for his early work integrating social media into journalism.

## Awards

In 2011, DeRosa was awarded **Best Innovation in Storytelling** by Reuters for his work folding social media into the traditional news article during his time as Social Media Editor at **[[reuters|Reuters]]**.

In 2013, he shared the **Journalist of the Year** award from the Spanish newspaper *El Mundo* with Jennifer Preston — then social media editor at *The New York Times*, later an executive at the Knight Foundation — presented in Madrid for pioneering work using social media in journalism.

## Press recognition

DeRosa's work running the Tumblr blog **[[soupsoup|SoupSoup]]** and integrating social platforms at Reuters drew coverage from a range of outlets: *The New York Times* called him "the undisputed King of Tumblr," *AdWeek* named him one of twenty "Young Influentials," *New York Magazine* included him among its "New Media Innovators," *Details* magazine named him one of twelve social-media "mavericks," *TIME* included SoupSoup on a list of must-see Tumblr blogs, and NBC New York named him one of the top 20 people to follow on Twitter.
