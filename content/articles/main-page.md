Welcome to this **personal wiki**, an encyclopedia you can edit.

It is organized like [[Wikipedia]] — with articles, [[categories]], [[backlinks]], and a table of contents on longer pages. Everything is written in Markdown with wiki-style `[[double bracket]]` links connecting related articles.

## How to use this wiki

Navigate using the sidebar or search bar at the top. Click any blue link to go to an article. Red links go to articles that haven't been written yet — click them to create one.

Each article has:
- A **title** and **summary**
- **Infobox** (the box on the right with key facts)
- **Table of contents** (auto-generated for articles with multiple sections)
- **Categories** at the bottom
- **Backlinks** showing which articles link here

Start by browsing the [[Getting Started]] guide or going directly to [[Special:All Articles]].
