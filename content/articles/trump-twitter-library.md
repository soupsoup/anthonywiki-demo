**The Donald J. Trump Presidential Twitter Library** is a *New York Times* bestselling book published by **[[the-daily-show|The Daily Show]]**, accompanied by physical installations of the "library" at locations across the United States. **[[anthony-derosa|Anthony DeRosa]]** helped produce the project as part of his work on the show's digital team.

## Awards

The installations won a Cannes Lions Grand Prix in the Outdoor category and a Clio Award.
