**Dr. Owen Birch** is a learning researcher and the first guest of [[pluralsight-podcast|The Meridian Podcast]], hosted by [[josh-burkhead|Devon Marsh]].

## Core ideas

Birch argues that most workplace training fails not for lack of content but because organizations measure the wrong outcomes — completion rates and satisfaction scores instead of behavior change and business impact.
