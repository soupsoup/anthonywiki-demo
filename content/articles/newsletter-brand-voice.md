**Newsletter Brand Voice** is the editorial philosophy and voice standard that governs [[pluralsight|Meridian Skills]]'s three newsletters — [[prompt-pluralsight|SIGNAL]], [[exploit-pluralsight|BREACH]], and [[scale-pluralsight|ORBIT]]. It is maintained by [[anthony-derosa|Marcus Reyes]] and documented internally as the program's editorial bible.

## Core purpose

The newsletters are meant to read like independent technology journalism — written for practitioners, not for marketing — even though they're produced inside a training company. Editorial independence from the marketing organization is a deliberate, documented stance.
