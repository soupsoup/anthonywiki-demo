**Filament** is a professional community platform built by [[tony-hale|Pete Vance]], former Chartbeat and Twitter employee. Marcus received beta access in early March 2026 through his longstanding connection with Hale.

## What it is

Filament organizes professional communities into topic-specific groups. The beta version Marcus accessed included an AI-focused group for tool sharing, groups for hiring and partnership conversations, and specialized channels. The pitch is structured professional community as an alternative to Slack — more organized, less chaotic.

## Meridian Skills integration potential

A possible use case discussed with [[chris-herbert|Diane Kessler]]: connecting course authors and learners after course completion, extending Meridian Skills webinars into ongoing interactive formats. The concern raised — preventing the platform from becoming a back-channel for authors to solicit consulting work or poach learners. No concrete use case was locked as of March 2026; Marcus noted he would write a memo if one crystallized.

## Status assessment

As of March 2026, Marcus found the value proposition still fuzzy. The "Slack but better" framing didn't fully land. Worth revisiting once the platform is further along or a specific Meridian Skills workflow emerges that needs it.
