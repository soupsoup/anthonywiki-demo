**Renata Cole** is Chief Marketing Officer at [[pluralsight|Meridian Skills]], appointed in early 2026 after more than a decade of B2B SaaS marketing experience. She reports to [[steve-vierra|Doug Hammond]], the company's Chief Revenue Officer.

Cole is a key partner for [[anthony-derosa|Marcus Reyes]]'s newsletter and content strategy — distribution and channel access for the newsletters and podcast largely run through her organization.
