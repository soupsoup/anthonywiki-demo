**SoupSoup** is a Tumblr blog created by **[[anthony-derosa|Anthony DeRosa]]**, known for curating a hard-to-categorize mix of news, photography, and video that built one of the platform's largest followings.

## Recognition

*The New York Times* profiled DeRosa as "the undisputed King of Tumblr," noting his blog had collected nearly 11,000 followers at the time and describing his posts as a mix that could include a photo of Lady Gaga before fame, a news item on internet wiretaps, or a parody of bad science reporting. *TIME* magazine later included SoupSoup on a list of must-see Tumblr blogs. The blog was also referenced in a segment on *[[the-daily-show|The Daily Show]]* with Jon Stewart.

## Career impact

SoupSoup's audience and reputation led directly to DeRosa's hiring at **[[reuters|Reuters]]** as the company's first social media editor — *New York Magazine* described the move as recognition of "serious unused editorial talent."
